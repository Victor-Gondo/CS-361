Stock Search Help

How to use the app

To use the search simply navigate to the search page and place in the values for the filters and the stocks with matching the requirements will be outputed

Filters

Market Cap

This is the literal "value" of the company which is calculated by multiplying the price of the stock by the number of shares that exist

Price

This is how much money you have to pay to purchase one share of the stock

Volume

This is the amount of shares which are being bought and sold in a day
